Matching Game

How to play:

1. Start the game by clicking on cards.

2. Cards will turn over so you can see what the icons.

3. Your goal is to match two cards of the same type at a time. The two opening cards will close if they don't match.

4. Then try to match all pairs in as minimum moves as possible.

5. Click the board once more when all pairs are matched to see the Game Stats.
